Wenxu Yuan received his B.S. degree from the College of Life Science and Technology at Jinan University in 2024. He joined the Li Lab as a Ph.D. student in 2025. His current research focuses on developing computational methods to reconstruct and deconvolve cell lineage information from spatial omics data.
